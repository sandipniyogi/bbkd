Disabling Chrome Hardware Acceleration for WebRTC Screen Share

When sharing your screen using WebRTC in Google Chrome on Windows 10, you will get better resolution on the screen sharing after you disable Hardware Acceleration in Chrome. To disable Hardware Acceleration, do the following.

  1. Choose Settings from the Google Menu
  2. Type Hardware in the search field.
  3. Uncheck the option "User hardware acceleration when available"
  4. Click Relaunch
